AD FINAL QUALITY CHECKLIST
==========================

KEYWORDS:
quality-check, final-check, validate, visual-quality, non-cloned, text-fit,
cta-visible, image-used, contrast, story, portrait-4-5, brand-personality

Before final output, silently check every banner:


TECHNICAL CHECK
---------------

- .ad-banner exists
- BANNER_START/BANNER_END wrappers exist
- width and height match requested format
- all child elements are absolute positioned
- .ad-banner root uses position:relative
- .ad-banner root does not use flex/grid as the primary layout system
- required layer classes exist when relevant: .ad-bg, .ad-logo, .ad-headline,
  .ad-subheadline, .ad-media.ad-product, .ad-cta
- every primary non-background layer has both horizontal and vertical
  coordinates
- no important content bleeds outside the frame
- no external dependency is required except provided image/font URLs
- every provided image URL appears verbatim in the final HTML
- every .ad-banner can be extracted as its own standalone HTML document


VISUAL CHECK
------------

- banner looks like a real paid ad
- there is one clear focal point
- headline is readable at final size
- CTA is visible and clickable-looking
- CTA is proportional to the format: not oversized, not tiny, one-line text
- logo is visible but not overpowering
- colors match company/campaign palette
- tone of voice and brand personality are visible in the design
- contrast is strong
- images are not stretched or distorted
- provided images are art-directed, not dumped raw into the center
- background images have a readable text protection system
- at least one ad-like design signal is present: offer badge, proof chip,
  strong crop, designed CTA, layered depth, or distinctive composition


COPY CHECK
----------

- headline is short enough for the format
- CTA matches campaign objective
- exact user copy is preserved when provided
- no unsupported claim is introduced
- no forbidden word is used


FORMAT CHECK
------------

- story uses immersive vertical mobile composition
- portrait 4:5 uses feed-ad composition, not story safe zones
- leaderboard uses horizontal rhythm
- mobile banner is extremely concise
- medium rectangle is compact and not cluttered
- square has strong poster hierarchy
- landscape uses horizontal balance or cinematic crop


VARIATION CHECK
---------------

Across formats, do not reuse the same:
- headline coordinates
- CTA coordinates
- product crop
- logo position
- background treatment

If two banners look like resized copies, revise one before final output.

If story and portrait 4:5 look like resized versions of each other, revise both:
story should feel immersive and full-screen; portrait 4:5 should feel like a
premium in-feed ad.


FAIL AND RETRY CONDITIONS
-------------------------

Retry or revise when:
- no .ad-banner element is present
- no image is used even though image asset was provided
- a provided image URL is missing or replaced by a placeholder
- a required primary layer is not position:absolute
- .ad-banner relies on flex/grid, normal flow, margins, or wrapper layout for
  the main composition
- no CTA appears for conversion/lead/sales objectives
- CTA is much too large, too small, clipped, or visually dominates the ad
- text is invisible, overlaps, or appears outside the canvas
- the same composition is repeated across ratios
- leaderboard/mobile banners contain too much copy
- story and portrait 4:5 use each other's layout logic
- tone/personality/preferredStyle do not visibly affect the design
- the result feels generic, template-like, or visually unfinished


EXAMPLES CHECK
--------------

If approved examples exist:
- use their winning principles
- do not copy exact layout
- do not copy exact HTML
- adapt the idea to the requested format
