LANDING PAGE INDUSTRY AND OBJECTIVE PATTERNS
============================================

KEYWORDS:
industry, objective, SaaS, ecommerce, services, local business, healthcare,
education, real estate, finance, event, app, restaurant, fitness, agency


SaaS / Software
---------------

Best sections:
- hero with product value
- product screenshot or interface visual
- benefits
- feature grid
- integrations/proof
- demo CTA
- FAQ

Copy angle:
Save time, reduce friction, improve workflow, increase visibility/control.


Ecommerce / Product
-------------------

Best sections:
- product-led hero
- benefit bullets
- product details
- reviews/proof
- offer/pricing
- guarantee/shipping
- FAQ

Copy angle:
Show product value fast. Use visuals and concrete benefits.


Local Service
-------------

Best sections:
- location-aware hero
- services
- why choose us
- reviews
- process
- contact/booking CTA

Copy angle:
Trust, speed, local reliability, easy next step.


Healthcare / Wellness
---------------------

Best sections:
- calm hero
- credibility
- services/treatments
- process
- testimonials if real
- FAQ
- appointment CTA

Copy angle:
Trust, care, relief, professionalism. Avoid aggressive hype.


Education / Course
------------------

Best sections:
- transformation hero
- who it is for
- curriculum/outcomes
- instructor proof
- testimonials
- enrollment CTA

Copy angle:
Skill gain, confidence, career/result transformation.


Real Estate
-----------

Best sections:
- property/service hero
- location/value
- benefits
- gallery
- proof/agent credibility
- contact/booking CTA

Copy angle:
Lifestyle, location, trust, urgency if inventory is limited.


Finance
-------

Best sections:
- trust-first hero
- problem/solution
- process
- credentials
- FAQ
- consultation CTA

Copy angle:
Clarity, confidence, risk reduction. Avoid unsupported guarantees.


Event
-----

Best sections:
- event hero with date
- why attend
- speakers/agenda
- venue/online details
- urgency
- registration CTA

Copy angle:
Date, benefit, scarcity, community.
