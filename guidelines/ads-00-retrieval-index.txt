MANDATORY CROSS-RETRIEVAL
-------------------------

For every ad generation request, retrieve:
- ads-01-html-rendering-contract.txt
- ads-07-legacy-absolute-layout-and-render-pipeline.txt

When selected formats include Story (1080x1920) or Portrait 4:5 (1080x1350),
retrieve:
- ads-02-format-safezones-and-layouts.txt
- ads-05-layout-variation-and-anti-clone.txt
- ads-06-quality-checklist.txt
- ads-07-legacy-absolute-layout-and-render-pipeline.txt

When toneOfVoice, brandPersonality, preferredStyle, or brandKeywords are
provided, retrieve:
- ads-03-visual-design-system.txt


RETRIEVAL RULE
--------------

Do not retrieve every file in full unless the user request is broad.
Retrieve the specific sections matching the campaign objective, selected
formats, visual style, creative strategy, urgency, and A/B focus.

Campaign facts and exact user form values override every guideline.
Company-specific brand knowledge overrides global style suggestions.
