AD FORMAT SAFE ZONES AND LAYOUT GUIDANCE
========================================

KEYWORDS:
selectedFormats, format, safe-zone, 1080x1080, 1080x1920, 1080x1350,
1200x628, 300x250, 336x280, 728x90, 970x250, 160x600, 320x50, 600x600

These are constraints and composition suggestions, not templates. Do not reuse
the same element positions across multiple ratios.


format=1080x1080 SQUARE
-----------------------

Safe zone: 54px all sides.
Best use: poster-like hierarchy, product stage, offer tile, bold headline.

Legacy absolute-zone starting map:
- logo: left 6%, top 5%, max-width 22%, max-height 10%
- headline: left 7%, top 12%, width 86%
- subheadline: left 10%, top 31%, width 80%
- product/hero: centered around top 43%, max-width 54%, max-height 34%
- CTA: centered, bottom 8%, max-width 72%

Recommended patterns:
- large focal image with headline block opposite it
- central product with angled badges and bottom CTA
- type-led poster with compact product/logo accent
- editorial split: one half visual, one half type/CTA
- offer badge: product plus discount or benefit callout

Avoid:
- tiny product in the center
- all text stacked in the middle
- using the same layout as 300x250


format=1080x1920 STORY
----------------------

Safe zone:
- top 250px
- bottom 250px
- left/right 54px

Best use: immersive mobile story.

Legacy absolute-zone starting map:
- background: full bleed
- logo: left 6%, top 6%, max-width 38%
- headline: left 7%, bottom 18%, width 86%
- CTA: left 7%, bottom 8%
- all primary copy below 65% canvas height
- no subheadline unless the brief absolutely requires it

Recommended patterns:
- full-screen image or product scene with strong gradient overlay
- hook in upper or middle third, product/hero in the visual center, CTA above bottom UI
- diagonal split, floating product cutout, editorial poster, or cinematic depth layers
- small urgency/proof badge near the headline or CTA when relevant

Story-specific rules:
- Do NOT treat Story as a taller feed portrait.
- CTA can sit lower than portrait 4:5, but never inside the unsafe bottom UI area.
- Use large mobile-readable type and strong vertical rhythm.
- Keep top/bottom breathing room for platform UI.

Avoid:
- horizontal split layout
- CTA too close to bottom UI area
- copying square composition
- using 4:5 feed-card structure


format=1080x1350 PORTRAIT
-------------------------

Safe zone: 54px all sides.
Best use: premium feed ad with more vertical room than square.

Legacy absolute-zone starting map:
- logo: left 6%, top 5%, max-width 25%, max-height 9%
- product/hero: right 4%, top 14%, max-width 58%, max-height 38%
- headline: left 7%, bottom 28%, width 86%
- subheadline: left 7%, bottom 18%, width 80%
- CTA: left 7%, bottom 8%, max-width 70%

Recommended patterns:
- editorial feed composition: brand/proof header, strong image crop, headline block, CTA near lower third
- product or service panel occupying 45-60% of the canvas
- asymmetric headline block with offer badge or proof chip
- image-led composition with readable text panel over/next to the visual

Portrait 4:5-specific rules:
- Do NOT use Story safe-zone structure.
- CTA should usually sit in the lower third, not at the extreme bottom edge.
- The ad should feel scroll-stopping in feed: polished crop, compact copy, strong CTA.
- Keep all copy within central safe area; images can bleed, text cannot.

Avoid:
- full-screen story-like CTA placement
- overly tall empty space above or below the message
- using the same layout as 1080x1920 Story


format=1200x628 LANDSCAPE
-------------------------

Safe zone: 48px all sides.
Best use: horizontal message plus strong visual.

Legacy absolute-zone starting map:
- logo: left 5%, top 7%, max-width 16%, max-height 13%
- headline: left 6%, top 28%, width 48%
- subheadline: left 6%, top 55%, width 44%
- CTA: left 6%, bottom 9%, max-width 35%
- product/hero: right 5%, top 12%, max-width 38%, max-height 78%

Recommended patterns:
- left copy/right product
- full-bleed cinematic background with lower-third CTA
- image-led crop with compact copy panel
- split canvas: image panel on one side, brand-color message panel on the other

Avoid:
- too many lines of copy
- square-like centered poster layout


format=300x250 MEDIUM RECTANGLE
-------------------------------

Safe zone: 18px all sides.
Best use: compact offer, single focal, one CTA.

Recommended patterns:
- top logo, central headline, bottom CTA
- product cropped on one side with short copy block
- badge + product + CTA in a tight diagonal rhythm

Avoid:
- more than one short headline and one CTA
- small decorative clutter
- long subheadline


format=336x280 LARGE RECTANGLE
------------------------------

Safe zone: 20px all sides.
Best use: medium rectangle with slightly more copy or visual room.

Recommended patterns:
- product/visual side crop plus CTA block
- headline-led card with small proof badge
- offer sticker near focal image


format=728x90 LEADERBOARD
-------------------------

Safe zone: 18px top/bottom, 28px left/right.
Best use: fast horizontal rhythm.

Legacy absolute-zone starting map:
- logo: left 4%, top 50%, translateY(-50%), max-width 120px
- headline: left 22%, top 50%, translateY(-50%), width 44%, single line
- CTA: right 4%, top 50%, translateY(-50%)
- product: optional small side accent only when it does not reduce readability

Density:
- headline max 4 words
- no subheadline
- CTA max 2 words

Recommended pattern:
logo -> headline -> offer/proof -> CTA

Avoid:
- vertical stacking
- large product image that leaves no room for copy
- more than 7 words of headline


format=970x250 BILLBOARD
------------------------

Safe zone: 32px all sides.
Best use: wide premium display.

Recommended patterns:
- large image crop on one side, generous copy on the other
- cinematic background with CTA on the far right
- modular panels across the width


format=160x600 SKYSCRAPER
-------------------------

Safe zone: 16px all sides.
Best use: vertical brand recall and simple action.

Recommended pattern:
logo top -> short hook -> product/visual -> CTA bottom

Avoid:
- long words that cannot fit
- dense paragraphs
- wide horizontal product images


format=320x50 MOBILE BANNER
---------------------------

Safe zone: 8px top/bottom, 12px left/right.
Best use: one-line hook and CTA.

Recommended pattern:
logo/icon -> 3-5 word headline -> compact CTA

Avoid:
- subheadline
- detailed imagery
- more than two visual elements
