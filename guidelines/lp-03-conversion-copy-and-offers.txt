LANDING PAGE CONVERSION COPY AND OFFERS
=======================================

KEYWORDS:
copy, offer, conversion, CTA, lead-generation, sales, booking, appointment,
download, waitlist, whatsapp, objections, audience


COPY PRINCIPLES
---------------

Landing page copy must be clear before clever.

Strong copy includes:
- specific audience
- specific offer
- concrete benefit
- proof or reason to believe
- clear next step

Avoid:
- generic claims
- hype without support
- long paragraphs
- too many competing promises


HEADLINE FORMULAS
-----------------

Use one of these when suitable:

Outcome:
"Get [desired result] without [pain/friction]"

Audience:
"The [solution] for [specific audience]"

Offer:
"Book/Get/Start [offer] in [timeframe/context]"

Differentiator:
"A better way to [job-to-be-done]"

Problem-solution:
"Stop [pain]. Start [desired result]."

OBJECTION HANDLING
------------------

Common objections:
- Is this for me?
- How much does it cost?
- How long does it take?
- Can I trust this?
- What happens after I click?

Handle objections with:
- FAQ
- proof
- process
- guarantee
- simple next-step explanation


URGENCY
-------

Use urgency only if provided or logically true.

Good:
- limited seats
- enrollment closes
- discount ends
- schedule fills up

Bad:
- fake countdowns
- unsupported scarcity
- aggressive pressure for premium/corporate brands
